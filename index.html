About Me, LLove Creator


Who are you?

I am Leslie Love, a visual artist exploring the digital realm. My multimedia works often combine nostalgic family imagery, found, thrifted, gifted, and natural handmade materials. The medium informs and inspires my art, as I leave wood grain, flaws, and unique qualities of materials exposed, revealing my concept and process. My work depicts connections and relationships between people and demonstrates my deep love and respect for the environment.
